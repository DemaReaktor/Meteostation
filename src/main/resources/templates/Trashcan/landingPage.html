Weather data Project of students of NTUU “KPI”.

What Insight™ Weather project is about?

Being the project of students, that study at the NTUU “KPI” (National Technical University of Ukraine “Kyiv Politechnic Institute”), it’s goal is to serve as a service, that provides collecting, processing, tools for restoring and displaying real physical data. This project is far from complete, but its development is currently opening up many opportunities for both students-developers and customers.
